Origami Tutorials

Origami Crane

Step 1: Start with a square sheet of paper.
Step 2: Fold diagonally both ways and unfold.
Step 3: Collapse into a square base.
Step 4: Fold side edges to the center.
Step 5: Create the bird base by inside folds.
Step 6: Form the neck and tail.
Step 7: Fold down the head.
Step 8: Pull out the wings and flatten.

Paper Airplane

Step 1: Start with a rectangular sheet.
Step 2: Fold the paper in half lengthwise.
Step 3: Unfold and fold top corners to the center.
Step 4: Fold the top edges inward again.
Step 5: Fold the airplane in half.
Step 6: Fold one wing downward.
Step 7: Repeat for the second wing.
Step 8: Adjust wings and test fly.

Origami Frog

Step 1: Begin with a rectangular sheet.
Step 2: Fold diagonally both ways at the top.
Step 3: Collapse the top into a triangle.
Step 4: Fold corners upward to create front legs.
Step 5: Fold sides inward toward the center.
Step 6: Fold lower corners outward for back legs.
Step 7: Fold the bottom upward.
Step 8: Fold back slightly to create a spring. Press and release to make it jump.
